FAQ
What is Staking? Staking involves locking up funds in a decentralized protocol. In return, stakers receive yields either in the form of additional tokens or through an increase in the value of their staked tokens.
How are the staking rewards funded?On DeDust.io, you can stake SCALE. The rewards for staking SCALE are funded through the distribution of fees collected by the platform.
What is SCALE?SCALE is a native token of DeDust.io, aimed at distributing the protocol's earnings via token staking.
How often are rewards distributed?Rewards are distributed daily, but sometimes it can take a bit longer. The amount you receive depends on your share of staked tokens and the volume of fees earned by DeDust.

Make note: only active SCALE stakers are rewarded. If you don't have staked funds at the time of distribution, you won't be rewarded for the period of staking since the previous distribution.
How can I unstake my SCALE tokens?Click Unstake and choose how to withdraw your funds. You can unstake them immediately with a 5% penalty or wait 72 hours to unstake without a service fee. A network fee is charged regardless of the chosen way of unstaking. It's a mandatory payment for processing your transaction in the blockchain.

Make note: along with the chosen amount of your tokens, the proportional amount of rewards will also be withdrawn.
